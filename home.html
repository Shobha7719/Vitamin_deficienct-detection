About VitaDetect

{# Moved this directly under the h2 #}
{# Added info-content-full-width here to constrain text #}

VitaDetect is dedicated to revolutionizing health diagnostics through cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Our mission is to empower individuals to gain quick and easy insights into potential vitamin deficiencies, simply by analyzing images.

Founded by a team of passionate researchers and developers, VitaDetect combines expertise in machine learning, medical imaging, and nutritional science. We believe that preventative health measures should be accessible to everyone, and our platform aims to provide a convenient, non-invasive tool to support this.

Our core technology leverages MobileNetV2, a highly efficient convolutional neural network, trained on a diverse dataset of images related to various nutritional markers. This allows us to offer predictions with confidence scores, helping users understand their health status better and encouraging them to seek professional medical advice if needed.

We are committed to continuous improvement, data privacy, and ethical AI practices, ensuring that VitaDetect remains a reliable and trustworthy partner in your wellness journey.

How VitaDetect Works

{# Moved this directly under the h2 #}
{# Added info-content-full-width here to constrain text #}

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Easily upload a clear image from your device's gallery or capture one directly using your camera. Our system is designed to process various image types for analysis.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Once uploaded, our advanced AI model (MobileNetV2) instantly analyzes the image. It processes visual cues to identify potential indicators related to nutritional deficiencies.

Step 3: Get Your Prediction

Receive an immediate prediction regarding potential vitamin deficiencies, along with a confidence score. This insight helps you understand areas where you might need to focus on your diet or consult a professional.

Step 4: Take Action

Based on your results, you can make informed decisions about your health. Remember, VitaDetect is a screening tool, and professional medical advice should always be sought for diagnosis and treatment.
